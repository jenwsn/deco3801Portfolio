Introducing, wanderlist.

Where will you wander?

Sustainable Tourism at your fingertips.


Wanderlist explores the problem space of ‘sustainable tourism’ where a mobile application is utilised to support the adoption of positive tourism behaviours. This behaviour is then incentified to promote consistency in practicing positive activities in relation to identified components in sustainable tourism. The application aims to utilise positive reinforcement to encourage behavioural change by listing sustainable activities for the traveller to explore, in doing so, the traveler will then unlock local discounts/benefits as a reward.
